DETAILS
The ultra rich volumizing mascara features a patented Phyto-Medic complex that delivers nutrients to feed lashes, rice bran that expands for maximum volume, and extended release technology that works overtime even after mascara is removed to repair, strengthen, and protect lashes. A trifecta created to guarantee longer, fuller, and healthier lashes.

HOW TO USE
See immediate results with a single stroke. Start at the base of lashes and wiggle brush up to the tip of lashes. Repeat to build.


No Parabens, Sulfates, Phthalates or synthetic fragrances.
